Individual Insolvency Register

Search the Individual Insolvency Register (IIR) for information about insolvencies and bankruptcies in England and Wales, including:

  • individual voluntary arrangements (IVAs)
  • debt relief orders (DROs)
  • bankruptcy restrictions orders (BROs) and undertakings (BRUs)
  • debt relief restrictions orders (DRROs) and undertakings (DRRUs)
You can search using a person's name or trading name (for sole traders).
! Warning Records are usually removed from the register within 3 months of a case ending.

About the Individual Insolvency Register

The Individual Insolvency Register is a combination of 3 registers:

  • individual insolvency
  • bankruptcy restrictions
  • debt relief restrictions

These registers are maintained by the Insolvency Service.

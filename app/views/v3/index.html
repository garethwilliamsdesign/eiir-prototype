Individual Insolvency Register

Search the individual insolvency register for details about:

  • bankruptcies
  • Individual Voluntary Arrangements (IVAs) and Fast-Track Voluntary Arrangements (FTVAs)
  • Debt Relief Orders (DROs)
  • current and interim bankruptcy restrictions orders or undertakings (BROs/iBROs and BRUs)
  • current and interim debt relief restrictions orders or undertakings (DRROs/iDRROS and DRRUs)

You can search the register by name or trading name (for sole traders).

If you notice any mistakes, contact the office or insolvency practitioner that dealt with the insolvency.
! Warning Records are usually removed within 3 months of an insolvency case ending.

Before you start

The IIR is an combination of the individual insolvency, bankruptcy restrictions and debt relief restrictions registers. The Insolvency Service is required by statute to maintain these registers, keep them up to date and make them available for public inspection.

About the Individual Insolvency Register

The Individual Insolvency Register combines the individual insolvency, bankruptcy restrictions and debt relief restrictions registers.

! Warning Records are usually removed within 3 months of an insolvency case ending.
If you notice any mistakes, contact the office or insolvency practitioner that dealt with the insolvency.

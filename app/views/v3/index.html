About the Individual Insolvency Register

The Individual Insolvency Register combines three registers:

  • individual insolvency
  • bankruptcy restrictions
  • debt relief restrictions

These registers are maintained by The Insolvency Service, which keeps them up to date and makes them available to the public.

If you notice any errors in the register, don't contact The Insolvency Service directly; contact the office or insolvency practitioner that dealt with the insolvency.

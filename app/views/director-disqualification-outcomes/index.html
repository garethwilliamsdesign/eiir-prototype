Director disqualification outcomes

View summaries and details of recent director disqualification results achieved by the Insolvency Service, or notified to us, including:

  • details of the disqualified individual
  • the start date of disqualification
  • the period of disqualification
  • details of misconduct

New results are published every week and can be viewed for 3 months from the date of publication.
